Her Story

Built on Trust, Driven
by Community

Glenda started her notary journey with a straightforward mission: make one of life's most necessary services as accessible and stress-free as possible. What began as a desire to serve her neighbors grew into a full-service mobile notary practice trusted by families, attorneys, real estate professionals, and businesses across Memphis.

"I wanted people to feel like they had someone in their corner — not just a stamp on a page."

As a fully insured, NNA-certified notary and licensed wedding officiant, Glenda shows up with calm professionalism for every signing — whether at a real estate closing table, a hospital bedside, or a courthouse ceremony. She also actively teaches community survival skills to Memphis youth, bringing the same spirit of service to the next generation.
