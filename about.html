Her Story

Built on Trust & Service

"I wanted people to feel like they had someone in their corner — not just a stamp on a page."

Glenda Cothran started her notary journey over ten years ago with a straightforward mission: make one of life's most necessary services as accessible and stress-free as possible. What began as a desire to serve her community grew into a full-service mobile notary practice trusted by families, attorneys, real estate professionals, and businesses across the Memphis area.

As a fully insured and NNA-certified notary public, Glenda brings more than credentials to every appointment — she brings calm, patience, and the kind of professionalism that makes people feel at ease during some of the most important moments of their lives. Whether she's at a real estate closing table, a hospital bedside, or a courthouse steps ceremony, her clients know they're in capable hands.

Over the years, Glenda added wedding officiant services to her practice — a natural extension of her commitment to being present for life's meaningful milestones. With four years of officiating experience, she has helped countless couples begin their lives together with personalized, heartfelt ceremonies across the Mid-South.

What truly sets Glenda apart is her unwavering belief that serving people means showing up — not just professionally, but personally. That same spirit drives her community work, her business, and every appointment she keeps.
