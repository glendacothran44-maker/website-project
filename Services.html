💍 Wedding Ceremonies
Be Prepared

What to Have Ready

To make your notarization quick and seamless, here's what to have on hand when we arrive.

Valid Photo ID

  • State-issued driver's license
  • U.S. Passport or Passport Card
  • Military ID
  • Government-issued photo ID

Your Documents

  • All pages present & unstapled
  • Do NOT sign beforehand
  • Blanks completed (except signature lines)
  • Any required witnesses present

For Loan Signings

  • Full document package from lender
  • Cashier's check if required
  • Two forms of ID recommended
  • Any co-signers present & ready

Good to Know

  • We cannot provide legal advice
  • Signers must be of sound mind
  • No coercion — signing must be voluntary
  • We bring all notary supplies

For Weddings

  • Valid marriage license in hand
  • Both parties present with photo ID
  • Any written vows prepared ahead
  • Two witnesses present (if required)
  • Custom marriage contracts available upon request
  • Family Bible recording of the event available
