Questions

Pricing FAQ

Payment is due at the time of service. We accept cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, and most major credit cards. Payment method can be confirmed when you book.
Each notarial act (signature) is $5 per Tennessee law. If you have several documents during the same appointment, the mobile service fee only applies once — making it very cost-effective to bundle everything together.
Weekend appointments during normal hours (8am–7pm) are the same price as weekdays. Only appointments outside those hours incur the after-hours surcharge of $15.
For standard notarizations, there's no time limit — we stay until everything is properly signed and certified. For loan signings, the flat rate covers the full package regardless of how long it takes.
We understand plans change. We ask for as much notice as possible if you need to reschedule. Cancellations with less than 1 hour notice on same-day bookings may incur a small trip fee. We'll always work with you.
